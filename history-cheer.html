History of Cheerleading

  • Cheerleading began in the late 19th century as a male-dominated activity at U.S. college sports events, evolving from simple chants to more complex routines.
  • By the 1940s, women became the face of cheerleading, introducing gymnastics, dance, and stunts into the sport.
  • Today, cheerleading is a global athletic discipline, blending acrobatics and performance with team spirit and competition.
