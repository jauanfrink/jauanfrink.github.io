Cheerleading Skills & Techniques

  • Jumps: Cheer jumps include moves like toe touches, pikes, and herkies, where cheerleaders leap into the air, aiming for height and form. Proper technique involves strong leg kicks, pointed toes, and good timing to achieve sharp, synchronized jumps.
  • Stunts: Stunts involve teamwork to lift and hold flyers in the air, with roles like bases, flyers, and spotters. Skills range from basic two-legged stunts to more complex tosses and basket catches, requiring coordination and trust among team members.
  • Tumbling: Tumbling includes acrobatic moves like cartwheels, handsprings, and flips that add dynamic energy to routines. It requires strong body control, balance, and core strength, with advanced moves needing careful progression to ensure safety.
