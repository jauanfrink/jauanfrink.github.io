Cheerleading Skills & Techniques

  • Jumps: Cheer jumps include moves like toe touches, pikes, and herkies, where cheerleaders leap into the air, aiming for height and form.
  • Stunts: Stunts involve teamwork to lift and hold flyers in the air, with roles like bases, flyers, and spotters.
  • Tumbling: Tumbling includes acrobatic moves like cartwheels, handsprings, and flips that add dynamic energy to routines.
